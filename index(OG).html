Welcome to KSS

Kagarama Secondary School (KSS) is located in Kicukiro District, Kigali City, Rwanda. Since its establishment, KSS has been officially recognized by the Ministry of Education as one of the leading government-aided schools in the country. Over the years, the school has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, discipline, and innovation in nurturing future leaders. Kagarama Secondary School began as an institution dedicated to providing quality education at both Ordinary Level (O’ Level) and Advanced Level (A’ Level). With a legacy of outstanding national performance, KSS has consistently ranked among the best schools in Rwanda. Today, the school offers a wide range of combinations at A’ Level, including PCM, PCB, MCB and MPG, ensuring that students are prepared to excel in sciences. KSS continues to uphold its mission of empowering students with knowledge, character, and skills to thrive in Rwanda’s fast-changing world.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures disciplined, innovative, and responsible citizens equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to contribute meaningfully to Rwanda and the global community.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel and positively impact society.

Our Programs

Ordinary Level (O’ Level)

At Kagarama Secondary School, the O’ Level section provides a supportive and engaging environment where learners build a strong foundation in both academic and social skills. Through a comprehensive curriculum and innovative teaching methods, students are equipped with the knowledge, discipline, and confidence needed to excel in their future studies and beyond.

Advanced Level (A’ Level)

The A’ Level section at Kagarama Secondary School offers diverse science combinations, including PCM, PCB, MCB, and MPG. With a strong focus on academic rigor and practical learning, this level equips students with the skills, knowledge, and problem-solving abilities needed to excel in sciences and pursue higher education with confidence.

School Facilities

Science Laboratory

Science Laboratory

Our science laboratories are fully equipped with modern tools and resources to support hands-on learning. Guided by a team of dedicated science teachers, students actively engage in experiments and practical activities that strengthen their understanding of scientific concepts and principles, preparing them to excel in both academics and real-world applications.

Computer Lab

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is a state-of-the-art facility that is equipped with modern computer hardware and software. It provides our students with hands-on experience in the latest technologies and tools, enabling them to develop their computer literacy skills. Our computer lab is supervised by trained technicians who ensure that our equipment is always in good working condition

Library

Library

Our library is a spacious and modern facility that is designed to provide our students with a quiet and comfortable space for reading, research, and study. It is equipped with a wide range of books, periodicals, and digital resources to support learning and research in various fields. Our library is staffed by qualified librarians who are always ready to assist students with their research needs.
