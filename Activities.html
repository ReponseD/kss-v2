School Activities

Engaging students beyond the classroom

Our Extracurricular Programs

At Kagarama Secondary School, we believe in holistic education. Our diverse range of activities helps students develop leadership skills, creativity, teamwork, and character while pursuing their passions beyond academics.

EAWSDC Debate Competition

Our students achieved second place in the prestigious East Africa Wide Schools Debate Competition, showcasing exceptional critical thinking and public speaking skills.

EAWSDC Debate Competition Achievement

We are thrilled to announce that our students at KSS have won second place in the highly competitive East Africa Wide Schools Debate Competition (EAWSDC). Our students impressed the judges with their exceptional critical thinking, research, and public speaking skills, showcasing their deep understanding of the topic and presenting compelling arguments.

This achievement is a testament to our school's commitment to providing high-quality education and fostering an environment that promotes growth and excellence. We are immensely proud of our students and look forward to supporting them in their future endeavors. Congratulations to our talented and dedicated students for this remarkable achievement!

Sports & Athletics

Our comprehensive sports program includes football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, and more, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition.

Sports & Athletics Program

At Kagarama Secondary School, we believe that physical education and sports are integral to student development. Our sports program offers a wide range of activities including:

  • Football and Basketball
  • Volleyball and Netball
  • Athletics and Track Events
  • Table Tennis and Badminton

Our teams regularly participate in inter-school competitions and have achieved numerous victories, fostering school spirit and healthy competition among students.

Youth Elevate Program

An innovative leadership and personal development program designed to empower students with essential life skills, entrepreneurship knowledge, and community engagement opportunities.

Youth Elevate Program

The Youth Elevate Program is a comprehensive initiative aimed at developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared for success beyond the classroom. The program focuses on:

  • Leadership and Team Building Skills
  • Entrepreneurship and Innovation
  • Community Service and Social Responsibility
  • Career Guidance and Mentorship
  • Personal Development and Character Building

Through workshops, mentorship programs, and hands-on projects, students gain practical experience and develop the confidence to become future leaders in their communities.

Annual Graduation Ceremony

A momentous celebration honoring our graduating students' achievements, marking the culmination of their secondary education journey and the beginning of new opportunities.

Annual Graduation Ceremony

Our annual graduation ceremony is a prestigious event that celebrates the academic achievements and personal growth of our graduating students. The ceremony brings together students, parents, teachers, and distinguished guests to honor the hard work and dedication of our graduates.

Each year, we recognize outstanding academic performance, leadership, and contributions to school life. The ceremony serves as both a celebration of past achievements and an inspiration for future endeavors, as our graduates embark on their next educational or career journey.

KWIBUKA Commemoration

An annual solemn commemoration where our school community comes together to remember, reflect, and honor the victims of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi, promoting unity and reconciliation.

KWIBUKA Commemoration

Each year, Kagarama Secondary School organizes a solemn day dedicated to KWIBUKA - the commemoration of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi. This year marked the 31st commemoration, where our entire school community came together to remember, reflect, and honor the victims.

The commemoration includes educational activities, testimonies, moments of silence, and discussions about the importance of unity, reconciliation, and building a better future. Through these activities, we ensure that our students understand the importance of history, peace, and the value of every human life.

This annual event reinforces our commitment to fostering a culture of peace, tolerance, and unity among our students, preparing them to be agents of positive change in society.

General Assembly - June 24th

A special gathering of the entire school community to celebrate achievements, share important announcements, and strengthen the bonds within our school family.

General Assembly - June 24th

Our General Assembly on June 24th brought together students, teachers, and staff for a special gathering focused on celebrating achievements, sharing important school updates, and reinforcing our shared values and goals.

The assembly featured recognition of outstanding students, announcements about upcoming events and programs, and inspirational messages from school leadership. These gatherings are essential for maintaining a strong sense of community and ensuring all members of our school family are informed and engaged.
