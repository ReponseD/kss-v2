Samuel Nkurunziza

Headmaster

Message from the Headmaster

Welcome to Kagarama Secondary School. As Headmaster, I am honored to lead a team of dedicated educators who are committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders. Our staff members are not just teachers; they are mentors, role models, and champions of excellence who work tirelessly to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

At KSS, we believe that quality education is built on the foundation of passionate, qualified, and caring educators. Our team brings together years of experience, innovative teaching methods, and a genuine commitment to student success. Together, we create an environment where academic excellence, character development, and personal growth flourish.

I am proud of our staff's dedication to maintaining the highest standards of education while fostering a culture of respect, discipline, and innovation. We are here to guide, inspire, and support our students as they embark on their educational journey and prepare for a bright future.

Mr. Innocent Bigiyobyenda

Dean of Studies (DOS)

Message from the Deputy of Studies

As the Deputy of Studies, I work closely with our academic team to ensure that our curriculum is delivered with excellence and that every student receives the support they need to succeed. Our teaching staff is carefully selected for their expertise, passion, and commitment to student-centered learning.

We have organized our academic departments to ensure comprehensive coverage of all subjects, from Mathematics and Sciences to Languages and Humanities. Each department is led by experienced coordinators who work collaboratively to maintain high academic standards and implement innovative teaching strategies.

Our teachers continuously engage in professional development to stay current with educational best practices and to enhance their teaching skills. This commitment to growth ensures that our students benefit from the latest pedagogical approaches and are well-prepared for their examinations and future academic pursuits.
