Welcome to Kagarama Secondary School

We are delighted that you are considering Kagarama Secondary School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to help us get to know each student and ensure they will thrive in our learning environment.

Admission Process

A simple, transparent process to join our school

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office or visit our school to learn more about our programs and facilities.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit all required documents including previous academic records.

3

Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance examination or participate in an interview process.

4

Acceptance

Upon successful assessment, you will receive an acceptance letter and enrollment instructions.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID
  • Previous school academic records/transcripts
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Medical certificate (if required)
  • Transfer letter from previous school (for transfers)
  • Parent/Guardian identification documents

Programs Offered

Ordinary Level (O' Level)

Our O' Level program provides a comprehensive foundation in core subjects, preparing students for Advanced Level studies. The program emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and holistic development.

Learn More

Advanced Level (A' Level)

Our A' Level program offers specialized science combinations (PCM, PCB, MCB, MPG) designed to prepare students for university studies in science, technology, engineering, and medicine.
