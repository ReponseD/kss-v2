Historical Background of the School

Kagarama Secondary School (KSS) is located in Kicukiro District, Kigali City, Rwanda. Officially registered with the Ministry of Education, KSS has established itself as a leading institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development.

The school began by offering Ordinary Level (O’ Level) secondary education, quickly gaining recognition for its outstanding performance at the national level. Building on this success, KSS expanded to include Advanced Level (A’ Level) studies, offering a wide range of subject combinations such as. PCB, MCB, MPG and PCM.
