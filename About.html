About Kagarama Secondary School

Who We Are: A value-driven, innovative school fostering excellence, integrity, and lifelong learning.

Kagarama Secondary School (KSS) is located in Kicukiro District, Kigali City, Rwanda. Officially registered with the Ministry of Education, KSS has established itself as a leading institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development.

The school began by offering Ordinary Level (O’ Level) secondary education, quickly gaining recognition for its outstanding performance at the national level. Building on this success, KSS expanded to include Advanced Level (A’ Level) studies, offering a wide range of subject combinations such as. PCB, MCB, MPG and PCM.

Historical Background

Kagarama Secondary School began by offering Ordinary Level (O’ Level) education and quickly earned a strong reputation for excellence at both district and national levels. Building on its academic success and growing demand from the community, the school later expanded to include Advanced Level (A’ Level) studies. Today, KSS offers recognized science combinations including PCM, PCB, MCB, and MPG, as well as the new Mathematics & Sciences Streams introduced under national education reforms.

Through continuous growth and a strong school culture, KSS has become one of the most respected secondary schools in Kicukiro District.

Our Mission

To provide a value-based, learner-centered education that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, integrity, and leadership, preparing students for higher education and meaningful participation in society.

Our Vision

To be a leading model school in Rwanda that inspires excellence, innovation, and character in every learner.
